Relative measurement units, such as:

  • rem
  • em
  • width
  • height

are computed through their closest parent.


For example Header's container's font-size is set to 20px

h1{ font-size:200%; } will give us 40px


Remember, that relative measurement units are computed related to their closest parent element..
